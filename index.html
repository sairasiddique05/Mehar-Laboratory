Diabetes Awareness

Diabetes is a common health condition that affects how the body processes blood sugar. Early detection and proper lifestyle management can help control diabetes and prevent complications.

  • Frequent urination
  • Increased thirst
  • Unexplained weight loss
  • Fatigue

Health Tips

  • Drink More Water
  • Exercise Daily
